BRYSON RECORDS

originally started as a small record label called 'Apex Recordings,' and then 'Phenomenal Records.' The Phenomenon released their debut album in early 2019 called 'Music for the Dark Ages' and the album also had an expanded edition. Bryson Records was also the chosen record label for Dise Phenomenon's solo project, called Dise. His solo project has made lots of singles, and eight albums, which are '2016', 'launchpad.', 'launchpad: deluxe.', '2017: Year in Review', 'Christmas 2018', '2018 (Includes Phoxling's debut track "Wonder")', 'The Tide' and 'paraDISE'.

The Phenomenon is currently working on an album called 'The Phenomenon 2.'

Our website is always being updated!
